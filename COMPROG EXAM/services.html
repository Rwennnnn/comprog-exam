Sedan:

A car with a fixed metal roof, separate engine, passenger, and cargo areas.

The Audi A4 is a luxury sedan that balances performance, style, and comfort. It typically features a 2.0-liter engine with 188 horsepower and front-wheel drive,
but a more powerful 248-horsepower engine with all-wheel drive is also available. The A4 offers customizable driving modes through Audi Drive Select and has dimensions
of 4762 mm in length, 1847 mm in width, and 1427 mm in height.

Coupe:

A 2-door car with a fixed roof, prioritizing style and speed.

The BMW M2 features a high-performance, turbocharged straight-six engine, M-specific design elements, and a range of advanced technology and safety features,
all while maintaining a compact and agile driving experience. It consists of a three-liter, turbocharged straight-six engine, a
Power of 365 hp, a Drivetrain Rear-wheel drive (RWD), enables transmission with both automatic and manual transmissions, and Acceleration of 0-100 kmph in 4 seconds.

Sports Car:

Designed for performance, handling, and speed, often with two seats.

The Porsche 911 is a high-performance, rear-engine sports car known for its iconic design and engineering, with a history spanning over six decades and multiple generations, all featuring a flat-six engine.
Horsepower is up to 388, a 9-hp increase compared with the 2024 model, enough to shave a tenth of a second off the previous car's time of 3.2 seconds. The Carrera S provides extra power and performance,
with its 473-hp version of the twin-turbo flat-six. It's only offered with an eight-speed PDK dual-clutch automatic transmission. However, the S also has the previous GTS model's upgraded brakes as well as a

Hatchback:

A car with a rear door that swings upward, providing access to cargo.

The 2024 Toyota Wigo rides on the Daihatsu New Global Architecture (DNGA-A) platform that underpins Daihatsu and Toyota's A-segment cars available in the region. In addition to being the new platform for compact
hatchbacks and crossovers, the DNGA-A platform is also built to accommodate electric and autonomous models. The Toyota Wigo in the Philippines is powered by an inline three-cylinder 12-valve 1.0-Liter 1KR-VE DOHC
gasoline engine with VVT-i, capable of producing 66 metric horses and 89 Nm of torque. These output figures are sent to the front wheels via a five-speed manual or a CVT.

Convertible:

A car with a removable or retractable roof.

technology. The Montero Sport is available in either a 6-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission. The SUV also comes with a Super Select II 4WD system that allows the driver to select between 2H, 4H, and 4L modes depending on the terrain.

Microcar:

The smallest size of cars, often with three or four wheels and small engines.

The Renault Twizy is a two-seat electric microcar designed and marketed by Renault. It is classified in Europe as either a light or heavy 2-door quadricycle depending on the output power,
which is either 4 kW (5.4 hp) for the 45 model or 13 kW (17 hp) for the 80 model, both names reflecting its top speed in km/h. It has a rear engine, rear wheel-drive layout.

Crossover:

A vehicle that combines features of cars and SUVs.

The MG ZS has a 1.5-liter gasoline engine producing 114 hp and 150 Nm of torque. The engine is attached to a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual. Power is sent to the front wheels.
For suspension, the ZS has MacPherson in the front and a Torsion beam in the rear. The stopping power of the vehicle comes from ventilated discs up front and solid discs in the back. The subcompact crossover is also fuel-efficient,
making you maximize the most out of your fuel. During our review, it was able to do 11.8 km/L in the city and 16.7 km/L on the highway, respectable numbers for its 1.3-liter engine. Another handy feature you might like is that it comes
with a 10-inch infotainment system that is compatible
with Apple CarPlay and voice-activated commands.

Limousine:

A luxury vehicle with a spacious interior, often chauffeur-driven.

The Mercedes-Maybach S-Class is the pinnacle of luxury sedans, offering unparalleled comfort and advanced technology. It features a 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine with EQ Boost, producing 496 hp and 516 lb-ft of torque.
The interior is adorned with premium materials, including Nappa leather and wood trim, and offers reclining rear seats with massage functions. Advanced safety features and a state-of-the-art infotainment system ensure a
first-class experience for both driver and passengers.

Luxury Car:

A high-end vehicle with premium features and materials.

The Lamborghini Urus is a luxury SUV that combines the performance of a sports car with the practicality of an SUV. It features a twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 641 hp and 627 lb-ft of torque, allowing it to accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 3.2 seconds.
The Urus offers a spacious interior with premium materials, advanced technology, and customizable options, making it a versatile and luxurious choice for those seeking both performance and comfort.

Pickup Truck:

A vehicle with an open cargo area and a cab for passengers.

The Mitsubishi Triton is a midsize pickup truck known for its rugged design and off-road capabilities. It features a 2.4-liter turbocharged diesel engine producing 178 hp and 430 Nm of torque, paired with either a 6-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission.
The Triton offers a spacious cabin with modern technology, advanced safety features, and a durable bed for hauling cargo, making it a versatile choice for both work and leisure.
